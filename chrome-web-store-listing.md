## Summary

Record smooth, cinematic scroll videos and GIFs of any webpage in one click.

## Detailed Description

Scrollywood turns any webpage into a polished scroll recording.

Choose a duration, pick WebM or GIF, and capture a clean, steady playback of product pages, articles, docs, portfolios, and long-form stories directly from Chrome.

Scrollywood is built for people who need fast, good-looking screen captures without opening a full video editor. It focuses on one job: recording a single tab as a smooth scroll sequence and saving the result locally to your computer.

Features:

- One-click capture from the current tab
- Adjustable scroll duration from 5 to 300 seconds
- Optional countdown before recording starts
- WebM and GIF export
- Automatic file download when recording finishes
- Capture polish for long-form and scrollytelling pages

Privacy and behavior:

- Scrollywood only records the tab you choose to capture
- Recordings are saved locally through Chrome downloads
- Scrollywood does not upload your recordings to a remote server
- Scrollywood does not require an account

## Suggested Privacy Tab Copy

- Single purpose: Record smooth scrolling videos and GIFs of the active browser tab.
- Data handling summary: The extension captures the user-selected tab and saves the recording locally via Chrome downloads. It does not transmit recordings or browsing data to external servers.
